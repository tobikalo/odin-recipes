Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna dough
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Stirr fry meat with medium heat
  2. Drown meat with tomato sauce, let rest for 10 minutes
  3. Add Cheese to the mix until you are happy with the consistency
  4. Stack layers of Mix with cooked lasagna dough, add cheese to the top of the lasagna stack
  5. Cook in furnace for 45 minutes
